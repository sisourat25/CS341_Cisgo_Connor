Help

- Click on any pin to zoom and see information at the location
- Click on any continent and country to zoom in
- Scroll or zoom in to zoom in
- Home button on bottom right to reset map

- Search the location, event, or location you are looking for
- Past filter will show you all the completed events
- Ongoing filter will show you all the ongoing events
- Show all will show you all the events
- Click on information card to showcase location information

- Click the add pin button
- Read through the instructions and make sure all fields are filled
- Click submit to submit your event
- A pin is successfully submitted once you see a success bar on the bottom

- Click the Log-in button
- You will be prompted an email username and password (which should be given)
- Once in the admin page, all pending and accepted submission will be shown

- The first table are pending submissions
- Check over the information to make sure all fields are valid (especially locations)
- Click the check on the left and click Change to Approved button to approve

Changing

- Fields are editable by clicking on the box
- Click the check on the left and click Change to Pending to remove from approved

Deleting

- Click the check on the left and press Delete Submitted
